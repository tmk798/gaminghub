How to use:
  • Click Flip to toss the coin and get Heads or Tails.
  • Use the result to decide who plays first or who serves.
  • Click Re-toss to flip again with a new random result.
  • Click Reset to clear the result and show a fresh coin.
  • Click Back to Home to go back to your main games page (set link in JS).
