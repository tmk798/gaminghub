Turn Order Allocation Game
Total Players:
How to use:
  • 1️⃣ Enter total number of players (max 26).
  • 2️⃣ Click Generate. Letters A, B, C... are created for each player.
  • 3️⃣ Each player chooses one letter (A/B/C...).
  • 4️⃣ Click Reveal on that letter to see a random unique number.
  • 5️⃣ That number is their turn: 1 = first, 2 = second, and so on.
Every round you click Generate, the numbers are shuffled again.
